Third Man — Cricket Scorer

Privacy Policy · Last updated 2026-07-09

What we collect

If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and Google account ID from Google, via Firebase Authentication. If you continue as a guest, we create an anonymous account with no personal information attached.

When you complete a match, a summary of that match — team names, result, and each player's batting/bowling figures — is saved to your account so it can appear in your match history and leaderboard. We do not collect ball-by-ball data, device identifiers, location, or any analytics /advertising data.

Where it's stored

Your account and match data are stored in Google Firebase (Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore). Match history is private to your account — our Firestore security rules only allow you to read or delete your own data, and no one else's.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell or share your data with third parties.
  • We don't show ads or use advertising trackers.
  • We don't use your data for anything beyond running the app's own features (match history, leaderboard, sign-in).

Deleting your data

You can clear your match history from within the app (Leaderboard → Clear history). To delete your account entirely, contact us using the details below and we'll remove your Firebase Authentication account and all associated Firestore data.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data can be sent to the app's developer via the contact details listed on this app's store listing.
